Enterprise Frontline Platform

Project Details

Client
T-Mobile
Industry
Telecommunications
Role
Senior Principal Product Designer
Focus
Enterprise UX • Design Systems • Retail Operations

Business Challenge

T-Mobile retail and care teams relied on complex enterprise tools to support customer accounts, upgrades, billing, payments, and service requests. The challenge was to simplify these workflows while creating a scalable experience that could support multiple product teams and future enterprise initiatives.

My Contribution

  • Led enterprise UX strategy and workflow design.
  • Designed complex interaction models and information architecture.
  • Created reusable design system components and specifications.
  • Partnered closely with Product, Engineering, and business stakeholders.
  • Validated solutions through iterative prototyping and usability testing.
  • Established governance to improve consistency across enterprise products.

Outcome

The platform established a scalable foundation for future enterprise products by standardizing interaction patterns, reusable components, and design governance. It improved collaboration between Design and Engineering, accelerated product delivery, and enabled teams to deliver more consistent user experiences across multiple initiatives.

Enterprise Design System

Design System at Scale

Built a reusable enterprise design system that standardized UI components, interaction patterns, accessibility guidance, and design documentation. The system improved collaboration across Design and Engineering while enabling faster delivery, greater consistency, and a more scalable product development process.

Enterprise Products Global Design System

Portfolio Note: This case study represents one of several enterprise initiatives I contributed to at Fidelity Investments. Three additional internal enterprise products are not included due to confidentiality agreements and company policy.

Project Details

Client
Fidelity Investments
Industry

Role

Focus
Enterprise Products • Global Design System • AI-Powered Design Workflows

Business Challenge

Fidelity's internal enterprise products support complex business workflows across multiple product teams. The challenge was to create scalable design foundations that improved consistency, accelerated delivery, strengthened accessibility, and enabled teams to adopt AI-assisted design workflows while maintaining enterprise quality standards.

My Contribution

  • Designed enterprise product experiences for complex internal business platforms.
  • Built and evolved a global design system supporting multiple enterprise product teams.
  • Created reusable UI specifications and component standards that improved consistency and accelerated product delivery.
  • Introduced AI-assisted design workflows that increased design exploration and team productivity.
  • Partnered closely with Product, Engineering, Accessibility, and business stakeholders to align customer needs with technical and regulatory requirements.
  • Established scalable design governance that improved quality, usability, and long-term maintainability across enterprise products.

Outcome

This work established a scalable foundation for enterprise product development through reusable design system components, standardized specifications, and stronger design governance. It improved collaboration across Design and Engineering, accelerated product delivery, and enabled teams to build more consistent, accessible, and AI-ready enterprise experiences.
